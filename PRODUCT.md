# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Immich owners who keep their main library private, and the friends or family who open a public share on phones and desktop browsers. Visitors need a familiar, low-friction gallery for viewing, downloading, and, when the Immich share permits it, contributing photos and videos without an Immich account.

## Product Purpose

Immich Public Proxy exposes only the content and capabilities granted by an Immich shared link while keeping the main Immich application and API off the public internet. Success means public shares feel dependable and photo-native at any album size, preserve the owner's Immich permissions exactly, and remain safe to operate without ongoing administration.

## Brand Personality

Calm, trustworthy, and warm. The product should feel like a focused photo experience rather than infrastructure tooling: familiar controls, restrained green accents, generous imagery, and concise language.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic admin-dashboard styling, security theatre, dense configuration surfaces, decorative glass effects, attention-seeking motion, and controls that compete with the shared photos. Do not imitate the entire Immich application or expose implementation details to visitors.

## Design Principles

1. Photos lead; chrome recedes until it is needed.
2. Immich share permissions are the single source of truth.
3. Familiar gallery interactions should work without explanation on touch and desktop.
4. Large albums and unreliable networks are normal operating conditions.
5. Privacy and failure states must be explicit; never imply an operation completed when it did not.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard operation. Preserve visible focus, semantic labels, reduced-motion preferences, touch targets of at least 40 pixels, and non-color state cues. Keep the interface fully translated through the existing English and Spanish dictionaries.
